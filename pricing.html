How does Workep pricing work?

The free version of Workep is available for teams of up to 10 members without premium features. Workep Growth Plan costs $9.99 per user per month when invoiced annually, and $11.99 per user per month when invoiced monthly. All prices are in US Dollars.

What payment forms are accepted?

Workep accepts any credit card. For annual subscriptions over 25 members, we can issue an invoice payable by bank transfer or check.

Are there special prices for non-profit organizations?

Workep is committed to humanitarian causes, we really believe in people who are trying to create a better and more equal world. For that reason, non-profit organizations receive a discount of 50%.

Do all members need to have G Suite?

No, you can have a domain to allow your teammates to signup themselves without invitations. But, you can invite Gmail users to your team too. It’s not necessary that they have G Suite.
